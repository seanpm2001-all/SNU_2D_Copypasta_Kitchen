Helpful features

Copy/pasteThe copyPasta Kitchen has a 1 click fix to copy a copypasta. Instead of selecting, and copying (or pressing CTRL+C/Command+C) all you need to do is click 1 button and the copypasta will be copied to your clipboard

Tips for beginners

Copying

The main point of a copypasta is to copy it around. You can simply right click and paste, and select the copypasta, but there are easier ways

The Old way

For Linux

Selecting

Method 1

Select the text with your mouse

Copying

Method 1

Once you have your copypasta, right click, and press copy

Method 2

Once you have your copypasta, press CTRL + C on your keyboard

Method 3

Once you have your copypasta, press the copypasta button

Pasting

Method 1

Go find an area to put it, click in, and right click and press "paste"

Method 2

Go find an area to put it, click in, and press CTRL + V
