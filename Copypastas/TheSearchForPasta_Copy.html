Note about reporting

We are going to make YOU look through it and give us the line numbers for the 2 (or more, hopefully not more than 2) lines that have the same value. This way you can't waste our time, as this system took severe loads of time to make. Be reasonable with your reports. It's on you!

Why are we even asking this? When there are thousands upon thousands of results (not there, but getting there) it is hard to spot duplicates. We rely on the community for small things hidden in vast distances sometimes and it is ok.
